Anonymous Blockchain Voting

SecureVote uses RSA blind signatures and a public blockchain to guarantee one-person-one-vote while maintaining complete voter anonymity.

The identity layer issues cryptographic tokens without knowing their content. The voting layer accepts only valid tokens — with no knowledge of who holds them.

How it works

  1. Register — Enter your Voter ID. The system verifies eligibility and issues a blind-signed token without ever seeing its content.
  2. Unblind — Your browser removes the blinding factor locally. The resulting token is a valid credential the issuer cannot trace back to you.
  3. Vote — Submit your token + candidate choice anonymously. The blockchain verifies the signature and records your vote (no name attached).
  4. Verify — Anyone can audit the entire blockchain and confirm the tally is accurate.

Security Guarantees

One Person, One Vote

Each Voter ID can only receive one token. Each token can only be used once (blockchain enforced).

Complete Anonymity

The blind signature protocol ensures no one can link your identity to your vote — not even the issuer.

Public Verifiability

The entire blockchain is public. Anyone can independently audit the vote tally and detect tampering.
